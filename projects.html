Buckeye Builder Android App

Buckeye Builder is a GPS-based game with goal of building the whole Ohio State Campus. Buckeye Builder is a fun way to explore Ohio State Campus and advance the game. Your task is to build the whole campus, and in order to do that you need money. In order to gain money and build buildings you have to physically go the building's location on campus and build the building.
