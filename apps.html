Buckeye Builder Android App

Buckeye Builder is a GPS-based game with a goal of building the entire Ohio State University campus. Everyone begins with a certain amount of money. After physically visiting particular locations on the campus, you can spend the money to construct buildings that will generate income to further construct the other campus buildings. This mobile application is a fun way to explore the Ohio State University campus while advancing in the game.
